Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how Altare collects, uses, stores, shares, and protects personal data when you visit the Altare website, play DreamRoyals, use Altare games, or interact with related services.

Last updated: April 20, 2026

1. Scope

This Privacy Policy applies to Altare Studio, the Altare website, DreamRoyals, and other Altare digital products and related support, marketing, and publishing activities, unless a product or service provides a separate privacy notice.

2. Information We Collect

Depending on how you interact with our games or website, we may collect:

  • Account-free gameplay data such as levels started or completed, move usage, booster usage, win or loss states, session length, and progression events.
  • Device and technical information such as device model, operating system version, app version, language, country or region, IP address, crash reports, and performance diagnostics.
  • Advertising and monetization data such as rewarded ad engagement, ad impression events, purchase status, refund state, and store transaction metadata supplied by platform partners.
  • Consent and privacy preference data such as whether analytics or personalized advertising has been accepted, refused, or updated.
  • Support or business communication data if you contact us by email, social channels, app store messages, or business collaboration requests.

3. How We Use Information

We may use information to:

  • operate, maintain, secure, and improve our games and website;
  • understand retention, monetization, level difficulty, ad performance, purchase funnels, and technical stability;
  • personalize ad delivery or ad measurement where consent and applicable law allow;
  • detect fraud, abuse, cheating, technical misuse, or payment-related issues;
  • respond to support requests, legal requests, and business communication;
  • comply with legal, tax, accounting, and regulatory obligations.

4. Legal Bases

Where required by applicable law, we process personal data based on one or more of the following: your consent, the performance of a contract or service request, our legitimate interests in operating and improving our products, or compliance with legal obligations.

5. Service Providers and Sharing

We may share relevant data with service providers that help us operate our games and website. Depending on the product and configuration, these providers may include:

  • Google AdMob and Google User Messaging Platform for ad delivery, consent handling, ad measurement, and privacy choices.
  • Firebase Analytics and related Google services for product analytics, diagnostics, and event measurement.
  • Google Play and other payment or platform operators for in-app purchases, billing validation, store operations, and refunds.
  • Hosting or infrastructure providers such as Vercel or other web delivery partners for website availability and performance.

We may also disclose information if required by law, to protect users or our rights, to enforce our Terms, or in connection with a merger, acquisition, financing, or asset transfer.

6. Your Privacy Choices

In supported regions or products, we may present in-app consent and privacy controls. You may use those controls to update analytics or advertising preferences where available.

You can also manage certain permissions and ad settings through your device, operating system, or platform account. Some product features, ad-supported rewards, or personalization features may be limited if you refuse optional processing.

7. Data Retention

We keep personal data only for as long as reasonably necessary for the purposes described in this Policy, including gameplay operations, analytics, fraud prevention, accounting, dispute handling, and legal compliance. Retention periods may vary by data category and service provider.

8. International Transfers

Our service providers may process data in countries other than your own. Where required, we take reasonable steps to use appropriate safeguards for international data transfers.

9. Children

Our services are not intended to knowingly collect personal data from children in violation of applicable law. If you believe a child has provided personal data to us improperly, contact us so we can review and take appropriate action.

10. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to request access, correction, deletion, restriction, objection, portability, or withdrawal of consent regarding certain personal data. To make a request, contact us using the details below.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will revise the “Last updated” date above and may provide additional notice where required.
